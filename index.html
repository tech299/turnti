Frequently Asked Questions

How does Turntu AI work?

Turntu AI converts your images into ASCII art by mapping pixel brightness to characters.

How do I adjust the size of the ASCII art?

You can adjust the scale and font size using the settings panel on the side after generating the ASCII art.

What is the character set?

The character set determines which symbols are used to generate the ASCII art. You can change this in the settings panel.

Why is my ASCII art so big or small?

Adjust the scale or font size in the settings panel to change the size of your ASCII art.

Our Methodology

At Turntu AI, we convert images into ASCII art by analyzing each pixel’s brightness and mapping it to a character in a customizable character set. Brighter pixels are represented by lighter characters (such as “.” or “-”), while darker pixels use denser characters (like “#” or “@”).

Our method captures the essence of the image while staying true to the nuances of light, dark, and color. This approach results in intricate works of art that reveal a new perspective on digital visuals.
